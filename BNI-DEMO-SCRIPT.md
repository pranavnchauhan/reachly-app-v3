## IMPORTANT RULES FOR THE DEMO

1. **NEVER mention:** Perplexity, Apollo, Claude, AI, artificial intelligence, machine learning
2. **DO say:** "our intelligence system", "our platform", "we monitor business events"
3. **NEVER show:** the admin panel, pipeline execution, niche configuration
4. **NEVER say:** "I manually validate the leads" — say "every lead is verified before it reaches you"
5. **The 3 leads are fictional companies** — don't claim they're real if asked. Say "these are representative examples of the types of leads our clients receive"
6. **If someone asks how it works:** "We monitor thousands of business events across Australia every week — acquisitions, expansions, hiring, new contracts. When a company shows a buying signal that matches your niche, we verify it, find the right decision-maker, and deliver it to your dashboard."
7. **If someone asks about price:** "Starts at $999 for 10 verified leads. Pay per lead, not per month. Let's chat after."
